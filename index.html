What is KOP of Boulogne ? ?

The Kop de Boulogne is an association of supporters of Paris Saint Germain. Our group of "ultra" supporters attends all the games played at the Parc de Prince and greatly contributes to the atmosphere that is so unique in this stadium, the "Parc des Princes".The KOP of boulogne sells tickets for upcoming matchs.
